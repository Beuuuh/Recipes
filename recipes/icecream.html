Ice Cream

A delicius ice cream

Ice cream is a frozen dessert based on dairy products such as milk or cream, to which fruit or other ingredients and flavors are added.

Most contain sugar, although some are made with sweeteners.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 can of sour cream with the whey
  • 1 packet of gelatin (your choice)

Step by step

  1. Make the gelatin following the instructions on the package.
  2. Put it in the blender along with the condensed milk and the cream.
  3. Mix well
  4. Pour into a container and place in the freezer to harden.
  5. Place in aluminum foil
  6. When the ice cream mass withstands, put it in the bowl of an electric mixer and beat until doubled in volume (about 10 minutes).
  7. Place in an ice cream container and return to the freezer until hardened.
