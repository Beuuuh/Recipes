Couscous

A delicious plate of couscous

Couscous is a typical North African dish, made with wheat semolina

It is usually served for breakfast or afternoon coffee and is a great replacement for bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of corn flour (corn)
  • 1 cup of water (approximately)
  • 1 level teaspoon of salt

Step by step

  1. In a bowl, combine the cornmeal with the salt.
  2. Mix well.
  3. Then add the water little by little and mix with your hands.
  4. Then cover this bowl with a cloth and let it rest for 20 minutes.
  5. Put water in the couscous pan (only half of the water compartment)
  6. Then put the steamer drain in the couscous pan and transfer the mixture to the couscous pan.
  7. Cover and cook over medium heat for about 15 minutes or until the couscous is firm.
  8. Turn off the heat, remove the couscous from the couscous pan and serve with butter and a cup of coffee.

You can also modify this recipe, you can add scrambled eggs and tomato, or put some meat and cilantro and it's delicious too!
