A delicious french fries

The term French fries refers to the product of the culinary preparation of potatoes previously cut into strips, very thin sticks, slices or leaves and subsequently fried.

This is an international food, very popular in the US with the rise of McDonald's.

Ingredients

  • 3 big potatoes
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • salt
  • frying oil
  • absorbent paper

Step by step

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them (without laziness) lengthwise as symmetrically as possible.
  2. Bring the strips to the fire with water and salt until they are slightly cooked.
  3. Remove them and drain very well, if necessary, dry them with a clean dish towel.
  4. While they drain, heat enough oil to cover the potatoes and add the unpeeled garlic clove.
  5. Now fry the potatoes in small portions and drain on absorbent paper. Serve it hot.
