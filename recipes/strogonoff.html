Strogonoff

A plate with strogonoff

Stroganoff is a dish originating in Russian cuisine consisting of cubes of beef served in a cream sauce.

Since its origins in the 19th century, the dish has become popular in many European and North American countries and in Brazil, always with considerable variations from the original recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2,20 lb of chicken breast
  • 1 onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 3 tablespoons of oil
  • pepper to taste
  • salt to taste
  • paprika to taste
  • 3 tablespoons of mustard
  • 2 tablespoons of ketchup
  • 1/2 sachet of tomato sauce
  • 1 can of sour cream

Step by step

  1. Cut the chicken into cubes.
  2. In a pan, place the oil, garlic and onion.
  3. Bring to a boil until golden.
  4. Then add the salt, pepper, paprika, mustard and ketchup. Now add the tomato sauce and let it boil for approximately 5 minutes.
  5. Turn off the heat and add the cream.
  6. To accompany this dish is rice and potato straw.
